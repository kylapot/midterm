Long Beach

Long Beach has a number of barangays that serve as starting places for public transportation, including San Isidro, New Agutaya, and Alimuangan. Long Beach also has a variety of restaurants that provide both local and foreign food. Long Beach is best explored by renting a motorcycle (habal-habal). If you're traveling in a group, you may book an e-trike to transport you to various places or join a day excursion in Long Beach.

Bato Ni Ningning

You must visit Bato ni Ningning to obtain the greatest views of Erawan Beach. Sunset is the most beautiful time of day. You must ride your motorcycle or rent a habal habal for 300 PHP round trip for two individuals. It takes around 20 minutes to get there.
