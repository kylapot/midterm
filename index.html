Start your vacation on Palawan.

Enjoy the ultimate island lifestyle in Palawan, the Philippines' Last Frontier. Discover its most popular places. This post will explain how to get about and the finest things to see and do there. With good reason, Palawan, one of the top destinations to visit in the Philippines, is frequently regarded as one of the best islands in the world. It is known for its white sand beaches and islets, pure blue seas, a diverse array of marine life and shipwreck sites, and dramatic towering limestone cliffs. If you enjoy swimming, island hopping, and diving, this Philippines' largest island province should be on your holiday itinerary. Palawan features almost 1,700 unspoiled islands and islets spread over its four main destinations: El Nido, Coron, and San Vicente.
